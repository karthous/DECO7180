Hamilton Caravan Park

Introduction

The Park with its off highway location, is quiet and peaceful and kept immaculately clean with well maintained gardens. Conveniently located adjacent to the Hamilton Indoor Leisure & Aquatic Centre, a state of the art sporting facility with heated indoor leisure pool, fully equipped gymnasium, squash/racquetball facilities and Cafe.
