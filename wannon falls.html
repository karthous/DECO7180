WANNON FALLS GATES & RESERVE

Introduction

The reserve remains relatively intact to its 1960s stage of development. The Wannon Falls Reserve is located south-east corner of the Township of Wannon. The Reserve straddles the Wannon River, and falls into the Parish of Bochara on the eastern side, being the major portion, and the Parish of Redruth on the western side.
