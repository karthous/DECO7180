Dunkeld Arboretum

Introduction

A sanctuary of walking tracks, giant red gums, lakes and even a labyrinth. The picturesque Dunkeld Arboretum allows you to take in the beauty, peace and imagery of nature at its finest. Enjoy a walk around the Arboretum tracks and view the ducks and swans playing in the ponds. The walking track is approximately 2km return. Relax and unwind with the family, pack a picnic and enjoy while taking in the breathtaking views.
