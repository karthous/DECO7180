Coleraine Caravan Park

Introduction

Coleraine Caravan Park is a council-owned caravan park offering powered and unpowered caravan and camping sites. The park also has toilet/shower block facilities and a coin-operated laundry. Bookings and payments are to be made via the Coleraine Post Office
