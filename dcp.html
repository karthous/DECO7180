Dunkeld Caravan Park

Introduction

A caravan park perfectly positioned at the foot of the Grampians, Dunkeld Caravan Park is located within the town and is a short walking distance to cafes, the Royal Mail Hotel, arboretum, museum, swimming pool, bowls club and tennis courts. Within the park there are a variety of cabins; two-bedroom villa, one-bedroom ensuite, budget ensuite plus powered and unpowered sites. All sites are reasonably large and a short walk to park amenities, including hot showers, toilets, laundry, park kitchen and much more. Dunkeld Caravan Park is the ideal base for wildlife watchers, outdoor adventurers and history buffs.
